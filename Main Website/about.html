About Us

Hand gesture recognition

Hello everyone, my name is Tahseen Begum and I'm the leader of this group. We're a team of four individuals who have come together to work on a project for our semester project.
Hand gesture recognition is an innovative web application that combines Hand gesture recognition with sign language. By analyzing the user's hand expressions in real-time, it provides personalized sign name. The provided code defines the structure and styling of the web application, as well as the JavaScript code for updating the Sign language dynamically. Throughout our time working together, we aim to maintain open communication, encourage creativity, and uphold a positive and supportive team environment. We are students from K L University, Hyderabad. We are BTech students from CSE branch (Honors).
